Chairperson

Mr. Pawan Mishra
Chairman, Jai Shri Ram Foundation Thailand

Mr. Pawan Mishra, a visionary businessman and philanthropist, is a Thai national of Indian origin who has carved a remarkable path in the world of business from his base in Bangkok. His entrepreneurial journey began with a strong foundation in marketing clothes, cosmetics, and jewelry for several prestigious brands. A turning point in his career came when he was mentored by a renowned gemstone business owner. Under this guidance, he mastered the intricate art of gemstone selection and soon rose to the position of Export Manager. Today, Mr. Mishra stands as the Founder & CEO of the PSG Group of Companies, an international conglomerate that operates across seven diverse sectors in five countries. His leadership is driven by innovation, ethical practices, and a deep commitment to philanthropy, empowering communities and industries through impactful initiatives.

Executive Committee

The Executive Committee at Jai Shri Ram Foundation leads with a vision to uphold Sanatan Dharma and deliver impactful community service across India and Thailand.
