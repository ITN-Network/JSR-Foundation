WHO WE ARE

Jai Shri Ram Foundation Thailand is a spiritual and cultural organization promoting Indian heritage, unity, and devotion through events, service, and community initiatives inspired by Lord Shri Ram.

WHAT WE DO

We organize spiritual programs, cultural events, charity initiatives, and educational support, spreading Lord Shri Rams values of dharma, compassion, and service across communities in Thailand and beyond.

MEDIA

Our media initiatives capture and share moments from events, festivals, and community work, spreading the foundation’s message of devotion, culture, and service through photos, videos, and digital storytelling.

CAUTION NOTICE

Beware of individuals misusing the foundation’s name. Jai Shri Ram Foundation Thailand operates officially through its website and authorized representatives only. Kindly verify before donation.
