About Jai Shri Ram Foundation

Overview

Jai Shri Ram Foundation Thailand is a spiritual and social organization focused on preserving Sanatan Dharma, promoting culture, education, and human values rooted in devotion and service.

A dream that lives on

Inspired by the timeless values of Lord Ram, we are dedicated to building a better society through seva, education, and community upliftment. We host events, support underprivileged children, and spread dharmic knowledge.

“True dharma is serving humanity with love, strength, and compassion.”

- Chairperson
